The Preschool


Nursery

    The School offers preschool programs (Nursery 1 and Nursery 2) which consists of activities and/or experiences that are intended to effect holistic developmental changes in children through an integrated and thematic approach prior to their entry into the basic education. The process to which the children learn will focus on:

Physical - development of psychomotor skills

Socio-Emotional - create an environment that will be motivated to develop self-esteem, independence, and maturity.

Cognitive - integrate subjects that will enhance the thinking, reasoning, and judgment by letting them discover new concepts and ideas, experiences, and situations and apply them in day-to-day living.

Kindergarten

    Section 8. Kindergarten Curriculum - Pursuant to DepEd Order No.32, s2012, DepEd shall continue to develop a Education General Curriculum (KEGC). The curriculum shall include standards and competencies expected of five-year old children along development domains. It shall focus on the child's total development according to his/her individual needs and socio-cultural background. The curriculum shall likewise promote the delivery of complementary, integrative services for cognitive development, health and safety care, nutrition, sanitation, psycho-socio-emotional, cultural and values formation activities; Provided, that it shall ensure to maximize at least three hours in managing the blocks of time with daily-planned Kindergarten activities; Provided further, that the curriculum shall be executed in a play-based manner through active learning experiences making children feel that learning can be enjoyable and meaningful.
    The curriculum shall take into account the distinctive features of children's physical, social, emotional, and cognitive functioning appropriate to their age, development stage and the culture values of their environment: family, school and community.

Grading System

Preschool and Kindergarten
Academic Honor [per Learning Area]
    At the end of the school year, academic honors are awarded to pupils who obtain the following grades per Learning Areas
      * Gold: 95.00 100.00 in final rating and no grade lower than A- in deportment in any quarter.

Overall Academic Honor
    At the end of the school year, overall academic honors are awarded to pupils who obtain the following grades in final rating.
      * Bronze: 85.00 -89.99 with a grade not lower than B+ in deportment
      * Silver: 90.00 -94.99 with a grade not lower than B+ in deportment
      * Gold: 95.00 100.00 with a grade not lower than A- in deportment

Contest Recipients
    At the end of the school year, certificates and medals/trophies are awarded to pupils who win in a contest.
